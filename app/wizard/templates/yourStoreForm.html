How to install GhostMonitor on your WP eCommerce site:

  • 1)In your WP Admin, go to Plugins > Add New.
  • 2)Search for “GhostMonitor" and click "Install Now" on our plugin. Alternatively, here's the direct link to the plugin on WordPress.org.
  • 3)Once the plugin has been installed, please activate it.
  • 4)Go to Plugins > GhostMonitor > Settings and paste your Site ID (listed above) in the applicable field. Click “Save” when you’re done.
  • 5)That’s it, you’re done!
